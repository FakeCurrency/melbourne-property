    Guide & key

    Every Greater Melbourne suburb (ABS SA2) is ranked against all others on each input (0–100, higher = better), then blended into two scores plus a slider-controlled overall.

    How to use it

    1. Pick a preset (or a mode) — the map recolours and the list re-ranks instantly.
    2. Read the map colour, or the ranked list in the panel.
    3. Click a suburb for its full scorecard.
    4. Tick ⚡ Show electricity network to overlay transmission lines + substations.

    The two scores

    • 🏠 Liveability — where to live or rent. Personal safety + family lead.
    • 🏗️ Development preliminary — where to invest/build. Headroom, growth, grid support.
    • ⚖️ Overall — the slider blends the two; a preset sets a starting point.
